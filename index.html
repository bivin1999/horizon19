About

Here's an awesome platform for you to brainstorm and come up with mind blowing solutions starting at the grass-root level, that could change the world. ISTE RIT Chapter proudly and excitedly presents to you HORIZON '19, a hackathon open to all BTech Students from all disciplines to unleash the innovators,creators and entrepreneurs.
The best team selected would receive a cash prize of Rs 7.5K and the runner up will be awarded with Rs 2.5K along with certificate of fame and glory.
So be there at Govt. Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, Kottayam on Aug 23,24,25 to give your brain a good workout and take it beyond the horizon.
All participants will be awarded with participation certificates and assured activity points for KTU students
